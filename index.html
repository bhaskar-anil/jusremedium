Our Approach

Confidentiality

The firm has comprehensive and secure information and document management process to protect the confidentiality of the client information. Information disclosed by the client to the firm will be kept strictly confidential and will be disclosed to its members only on a need to know basis.

Conflict

The firm guarantees to its clients that all internal enquiries would be made in order to ascertain the conflict of interest in relation to any transaction and the client will be immediately notified of a situation which may lead to a conflict of interest throughout the duration of the transaction.
