Secretarial Compliance

With the coming of new age companies laws in India, the secretarial compliance for companies have become quite stringent and need to be addressed timely and efficiently. Firm has a team of company secretaries who assist domestic and international clients in setting up their companies and handling their secretarial and regulatory compliance.

The scope of work for secretarial services for our clients includes:

  • Preparation of draft agenda/ notices convening the meetings of the board of directors/ shareholders.
  • Preparation of draft resolutions and minutes of meetings of the board of directors and of shareholders in the ordinary and routine course of business.
  • Preparation of documents in relation to statutory filings, that is, Form 20B, Form 23AC and 23 ACA, Form DIR - 12, Form PAS - 3, Form SH-7, Form MGT -14 etc. as prescribed under the Companies Act, 2013 (Act) and arranging filings of the same with the Registrar of Companies (ROC).
  • Preparation of Annual Return and Directors Report and arranging filings of the same with the ROC.
  • Preparation of notice of disclosure of interest of a director in Form MBP – 1, Form DIR - 8 as prescribed under the Act.
  • Advising on convergence from Companies Act, 1956 to Companies Act 2013.
