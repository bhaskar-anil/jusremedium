Litigation

Firm handles most aspects of dispute resolution, with a niche focus on international commercial litigation before courts and arbitral tribunals. Firm’s litigators provide a full range of litigation and arbitration services at all levels for both local and international clients.

Original side litigation expertise of the Firm varies from suit for recoveries, specific performance of contracts, declarations & injunctions, cancellation and delivery-up of documents, partition suits and matrimonial & family settlement litigation at all district courts in any jurisdiction, and appeals arising from the above suits before the High Court and other forums including Consumer Dispute Redressal Forum, Intellectual Property Appellate Board, Company law Board, BIFR, AAIFR and MRTP.
